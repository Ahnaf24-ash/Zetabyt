Privacy Policy – ZETABYT

Last updated on: 7th April 2026

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all ZETABYT websites, products, and services that link to it.

Our Privacy Commitment

At ZETABYT, we respect your privacy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for advertising or profit. Our goal is to collect only the minimum information necessary to deliver reliable IT services and improve user experience.

This policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How we use it
  • Who can access it
  • Your rights regarding your data

Part I – Information ZETABYT Collects and Controls

What Information We Collect

We collect information only when necessary, in the following ways:

Information You Provide
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company details (if applicable)
  • Messages or inquiries via forms or email
Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL

This helps us improve website performance and user experience.

Information from Third Parties

We may receive limited data from:

  • Social media platforms (if you interact with us)
  • Authentication providers (like Google login, if used)
  • Referral or partner programs

What We Do With Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Send updates, offers, or important notifications
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on:

  • Your consent
  • Service requirements (to deliver requested services)
  • Legitimate business interests

Your Choices & Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of marketing emails anytime
  • Disable cookies through your browser
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data

To make a request, contact us at:
info@ZETABYT.in

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

All partners must follow strict data protection standards.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for:

  • Service delivery
  • Legal obligations

After that, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Data is deleted after account termination within a reasonable timeframe.
